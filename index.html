About Us

Innovation in Renewable Energy & Climate Change

Innovation in Renewable Energy & Climate Change" refers to the development and implementation of new technologies, practices, and solutions that harness renewable energy sources like solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and tidal power to combat climate change by reducing reliance on fossil fuels and their associated greenhouse gas emissions; essentially, it's about finding new and improved ways to generate clean energy to mitigate the impacts of climate change.
